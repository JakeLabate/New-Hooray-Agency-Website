Account Coordinator

Overview /

The Account + Marketing Coordinator will work across multiple departments, supporting various stakeholders within the agency. In this role, you will partner with account leaders to support the day-to-day success of your assigned group of accounts. If you are curious, courageous, and optimistic by nature. Every day will look different, so you are ready to adapt and work well under pressure. Never afraid to speak your mind, you’re a natural communicator and always looking for a new challenge.

Responsibilities /

  • Work to understand the business and account needs for each client within your portfolio
  • Build strong working relationships with account team members, production team & clients
  • Use project management software to manage daily processes and approvals
  • Own all client deliverables, ensuring projects are completed on-time and within scope
  • Act as communication point between clients & internal production team
  • Anticipate upcoming needs and proactively mitigate any issues
  • Generate and deliver monthly reporting for each client
  • Provide administrative support, including meeting scheduling and hours tracking
  • Take an active role in the strategic development and guidance on your accounts

Required Skills /

  • Have a Bachelor’s Degree or relevant working experience.
  • Previous experience in the hospitality industry, particularly in marketing and revenue management within hotel environments.
  • Demonstrate strong communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Prior agency experience is highly valued, along with an extensive background in hospitality operations.
  • Preference for candidates with a background in digital media marketing.
  • Experience in team or group management roles
